📘 About Student Hub

Supporting Year 13 Fiji Students

🎯 Our Mission

Student Hub was created to provide free, structured, and accessible learning resources for Year 13 students in Fiji. Our goal is to help students prepare effectively for final examinations through past papers, study tools, and subject guides.

👨‍💻 Who We Are

This platform is designed by students/educators who understand the challenges of Year 13. We aim to make learning simpler, clearer, and more accessible for all students regardless of location.

📚 What We Provide

🔵 Physics revision resources
🟢 Maths past papers and notes
🔴 English essay and comprehension help
🟠 Computer Studies tutorials
⚫ Technical Drawing guides

🤝 Get Involved

Students can contribute by suggesting resources, sharing study tips, or helping improve the platform. This hub grows through collaboration and student feedback.

🚀 Our Goal

To ensure every Year 13 student in Fiji has equal access to quality revision materials, helping them succeed in their final exams.
