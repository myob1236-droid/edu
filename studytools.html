💡 Study Tips for Year 13 Exams

  • 📌 Do past papers under timed conditions
  • 📌 Focus on weak topics first
  • 📌 Study in short 30–45 min sessions
  • 📌 Use active recall instead of rereading notes
  • 📌 Mark your answers using marking guides
  • 📌 Revise daily, not last minute
